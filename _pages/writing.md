## Bioinformatics: A Poetry Collection  

<img src="/images/book_cover.png" alt="Poetry Collection Preview" width="400"/>

**Description:**  
This is a scientic poetry collection exploring research in different areas of bioinformatics. The main goal of this collection was to make science less intimidating and more accessible. By blending art and science, these poems help people see the beauty behind what we do in bioinformatics because science isn’t just about facts and figures—it’s about creativity, problem-solving, and discovering the unknown These poems aim to capture that and make it relatable for anyone, no matter their background.

## Sfumato: A Tragedy (Novel)

**Description:**  
Sfumato is a literary fiction novel following a young adult's life through his diary entries. This book offers an unflinching look at human suffering, exploring grief, suicidal ideation, self-harm, and depression without looking away. 

In art, sfumato is the technique of blurring edges between light and shadow. In this book, it represents the slow erosion of identity as our character navigates loss of meaning and connection.

This project is dedicated to people who are suffering silently, those for whom suicide and self-harm have been part of life. I believe these internal realities deserve a voice that doesn't flinch, and the people living them deserve to be seen.
